One octal digit = 3 bits

# Converting [[Denary (Decimal)]] to [[Octal]]
$\Large{12858_{10}} = 31072_{8}$

$$
\begin{array}{|r|r|r|r|}
\hline
4096=8^4 & 512=8^3 & 64=8^2 & 8=8^1 & 1=8^0 \\
\hline
3 & 1 & 0 & 7 & 2 \\
\hline
\end{array}
$$

$$
(3 \times 4096) +
(1 \times 512) +
(0 \times 64) +
(7 \times 8) +
(2 \times 1)
= 12858
$$
